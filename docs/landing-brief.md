# ETHA Landing Page Redesign Brief

*One story. Seven sections. One action.*

---

## Context

| | |
|---|---|
| **Traffic** | Instagram and Facebook ads, organic social |
| **Goal** | Take the Dosha quiz |
| **Device** | Mobile-first (390px) |
| **First 5 seconds** | She feels seen, not sold to |

---

## Global Design Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Aura** | Every section must include the Etha Aura. It is the brand. Its absence is a failure. |
| **One CTA** | "Begin your remembering." Always. Never vary the wording. Never add a competing CTA. |
| **Palette** | Aubergine (#3D233B) and Cream (#FFEFDE) only. No Dosha antidote colours as universal accents. |
| **Mobile first** | Design at 390px viewport first. She is on her phone. |
| **Typography** | Plantin for all copy. Brandon Grotesque uppercase for labels only. Nothing else. |
| **Space** | Generous space is not empty. It is the brand breathing. Do not fill it. |
| **No stock photo** | If no approved ETHA photography exists, use the Aura and type to carry the visual weight. |
| **Quiet authority** | ETHA does not shout. It whispers with total conviction. Never compete for attention. |

---

## What Must Be Removed (from current page)

| Item | Why |
|---|---|
| **Product shop mid-page** | She cannot be asked to buy before she knows her Dosha. Remove the ADD TO CART grid entirely. |
| **Rituals section** | Does not serve the quiz narrative. Move to a dedicated rituals page. |
| **Academy stat grid** | "50+ experts, 3k+ hours" reads like a SaaS page. Strip it entirely. |
| **8 competing CTAs** | The page pulls Joana in 8 directions. One primary CTA only: "Begin your remembering." |
| **Hero autoplay video** | Breaks the stillness. Remove from above the fold. User-initiated only if kept at all. |
| **SHOP navigation link** | Competes with the quiz CTA. The nav on this page exists only to carry the logo. |

## Section 01 — Hero

**Background:** Aubergine

| | |
|---|---|
| **What needs to happen** | She lands from Instagram or an ad. Show her something different. Quiet. Still. Confident. No selling. |
| **Main message** | *"Return to the source"* |
| **How she feels** | This is different. This is for me. |
| **What she does** | She pauses. Reads slowly. Scrolls down. |
| **CTA** | Begin your remembering |
| **Aura state** | Balanced Aura, large, fills the background |
| **Design rule** | Full viewport height. No hero video above the fold. Headline in Plantin Semi Bold, very large. Cream on Aubergine. |

**Current page issue:** Remove the autoplay video. It breaks the stillness that should define her first impression.

### Joana Reacts — Clear Pass

| She thinks | She feels | She does |
|---|---|---|
| *"That's not what I expected from a wellness brand. Usually it's 'unlock your best self' or something equally exhausting."* | A small exhale. Suspicious still, but my finger pauses. | I don't scroll immediately. I read it again. |

## Section 04 — Quiz Invitation

**Background:** Aubergine

| | |
|---|---|

| **Main message** | *"This is not a trend. It is your bio-individual blueprint."* |

### Joana Reacts — Watch One Phrase

| She thinks | She feels | She does |
|---|---|---|
| *"'Bio-individual blueprint' is a little much. But 'this is not a trend' stops me. That's exactly what I needed someone to say."* | Permission. The subtle kind. Like someone just told me I'm allowed to slow down. | I read it twice. I hover over the button. I click it. |

> **Warning:** Watch "bio-individual blueprint". It reads slightly clinical and briefly pulls her out. The rest of the section earns the click despite it.
